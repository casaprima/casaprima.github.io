Belvedere

¶ This was the first personal project that I designed while I was at Coppers and Brasses, and actually the first font I designed. It started out as a couple of TypeCooker sketches and a desire to make a script font. Good thing that Alex and Étienne advised me against it (and that I listened to them). Still, I remained somewhat close to the first idea, ending up with a kind of curvy broad-nib-contrast italic.
